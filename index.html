Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Reel Canister ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Email address (for account creation and sign-in)
  • URLs and titles of reels you save
  • Canister (folder) names you create
  • Subscription status (free or pro)

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or physical address
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the Reel Canister service — saving, organising, and retrieving your reels. AI categorisation is performed using your reel titles and captions to suggest the best canister.

Third-party services

  • Supabase – database and authentication
  • Stripe – payment processing (we never store card details)
  • OpenAI – AI-powered categorisation

Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen.
